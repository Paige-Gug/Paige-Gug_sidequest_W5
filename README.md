## Project Title

GBDA302 Week 5 Sidequest - Meditative camera experience

---

## Authors

Paige Gugeler, Karen Cochrane and David Han

---

## Description

This game allows the player to scroll through a world where the sun is setting. Through discovered text and design elements, it encourages the player to reflect on their day and wind down for sleep.

---

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

The game automatically starts the player just before sunset. The player can then use the WASD or arrow keys to move through the world to nightfall.
